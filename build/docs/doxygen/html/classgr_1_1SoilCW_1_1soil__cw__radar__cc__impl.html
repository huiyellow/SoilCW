Class Constructor. The jobs include:

  1. Initialise sample buffers
    See also
    init_sample_buffers()
  2. Create an instance of BladerfDevice, and set it to use two TX and two RXs, one pair for radar channel, one pair for reference channel
  3. Provide frequency plan to the device to enable quick tune functionality
    Parameters
    main_freqThe main frequency of the radar in Hz
    delta_fThe frequency difference in Hz
    samp_rateSampling rate in Hz
    rx_gainThe gain of the radar RX channel
    tx_gainThe gain of the radar TX channel
    ref_gainThe gain of the reference TX and RX channel
    enable_biasteeSet the flag if BT-100 and BT-200 are connected with radar TX and radar RX channels
    baseband_ampThe amplitude of the baseband pulse
    baseband_freqThe frequency of the baseband pulse
